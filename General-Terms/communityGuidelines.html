Community Guidelines

Creating a Safe, Respectful, and Productive Digital Ecosystem

These guidelines ensure that all Archivehubs.com users engage in a responsible, professional, and collaborative manner. By participating in the Archivehubs community, you agree to uphold these standards

1. Respect & Inclusivity

  • Treat all users with dignity, respect, and kindness
  • Discrimination, harassment, hate speech, or abusive behavior is strictly prohibited
  • Archivehubs is an inclusive space welcoming people of all backgrounds, abilities, and identities

2. Content Integrity

  • Upload only digital assets you own or have rights to share
  • Misleading, plagiarized, or stolen content will be removed and may result in account suspension
  • Properly tag and categorize your files for accuracy and discoverability

3. No Harmful or Illegal Use

  • Do not use Archivehubs.com to store or distribute malware, explicit content, or illegal material
  • Spam, scams, or attempts to exploit others will not be tolerated

4. Professional Conduct

  • Collaborate professionally in comments, messages, and shared workspaces
  • Respect intellectual property and confidentiality of others
  • Avoid self-promotion in areas meant for community engagement unless clearly allowed

5. Community Engagement

  • Constructively contribute to discussions and shared projects
  • Report abuse, copyright violations, or suspicious behavior to the moderation team
  • Participate in feedback sessions, tutorials and learning labs to grow the community

6. Privacy & Security

  • Do not share another user's personal or confidential information without consent
  • Use Archivehubs' privacy settings to manage who sees your content and collaborations
  • Protect your account with strong passwords and two-factor authentication

7. Enforcement & Violations

  • Archivehubs reserves the right to moderate, suspend, or terminate accounts violating these guidelines
  • Users may receive warnings, content removal, or be banned depending on the severity of the breach
  • Repeated violations will lead to permanent suspension

Questions or concerns?

Visit our Safety Center or Contact Support for help or to report an issue

Manage billing & account details

Easily update your payment information, view invoices, and control your subscription preferences - all in one place.

Billing Management Features

View & Download Invoices

  • Access your billing history
  • Download PDF copies of all past invoices and receipts
  • Filter by date, service type, or plan

Update Payment Method

  • Add, remove, or update credit/debit cards or other payment options
  • Set default payment method for auto-renewals

Subscription Plan Management

  • Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time
  • View included features and available add-ons
  • Check remaining storage and service credits

Billing Notifications & Alerts

  • Receive alerts for upcoming charges, failed payments, or renewal reminders
  • Manage email preferences for billing communications

Account Details Management

Edit Profile Information

  • Update personal, company or organization name, email, contact number, and address

Change Password & Security Settings

  • Reset login credentials
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • View recent login activity

Linked Accounts & Integrations

  • Manage connected third-party services (e.g. Google, Microsoft, PayStack)
  • Authorize or revoke API access

Deactivate or Delete Account

  • Temporarily suspend your account
  • Permanently delete your profile and associated data (with verification)

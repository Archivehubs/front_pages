Monetization Policy

Turning Digital Assets into Real Value

At Archivehubs.com, users can monetize their creative and professional digital assets - such as documents, media, templates, reseaarch, designs, and more. This policy outlines the rules, eligibility, and expectations for earning through the platform

1. Eligibility for Monetization

To qualify for monetization, users must

  • Have a verified Archivehubs.com account
  • Upload original content or content they have full legal rights to distribute
  • Agree to the Archivehubs Terms of Service, Copyright Policy and this monetization Policy
  • Maintain a good standing (i.e, no violations of community guidelines)

2. Monetizable Content Types

Users may earn through:

  • Digital Asset Sales: Sell ebooks, templates, PRDs, training materials, photography, videos, etc.
  • Licensing: Offer content for one-time or recurring licensing to individuals or organizations.
  • Affiliate Referrals: Earn commissions for bringing new users via the Affiliate program.
  • Sponsored Content & Ads: Monetize visibility through promoted content (available to approved users)
  • Open Market Sales or Licensing: Sell or license other physical assets via Archivehubs.com open market channels.

3. Pricing & Commission Structure

  • Users set their own prices for digital or physical assets.
  • Archivehubs.com retains an affiliate fee (between 5% - 15%, depending on your activities).
  • Payouts are made monthly upon reaching a minimum threshold of $50 or N75,000.
  • All transactions are transparent and viewable in the Monetization hub.

Direct Marketing Commission with Archivehubs.com

  • Users set their own prices for digital assets and services while Archivehubs.com retains 30% commission of thetransactions
  • Archivehubs.com retain the outline commissions depending on the transaction for physical products i.e;

4. Content Requirements

To be eligible for monetization, content must:

  • Be original and not infringe any copyrights or trademarks
  • Be clearly described,categorized, and tagged
  • Follow ethical standards (no hate, violence, or illegal subject matter)
  • Offer real value to buyers (educational, creative or professional use)

5. Reporting & Analytics

  • Creators can access dashboards to view performance metrics: downloads, earnings, and buyer interactions
  • Revenue reports can be downloaded for accounting and tax purposes

6. Security & Fraud Protection

  • All sales and licensing transactions are protected by secure payment gateways
  • Fradulent accounts or content will be suspended without notice
  • Refunds and chargebacks are reviewed on a case-by-case basis per Archivehubs policy

7. Prohibited Monetization Practices

Users may not:

  • Upload third-party content without permission
  • Use bots or false engagement tactics
  • Misrepresent content with misleading tags or titles
  • Circumvent fees or redirect users off-platform to avoid payments

Violations may lead to earnings forfeiture or account termination

8. Payout & Tax Compliance

  • Payouts are made via approved channels (e.g. PayStack,bank transfer)
  • Users are responsible for any applicable taxes in their jurisdiction
  • Archivehubs may request tax identification information for legal complaince

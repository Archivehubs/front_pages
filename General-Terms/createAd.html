Create Ad

Overview

The "Create Ad" feature empowers users, businesses, and content creators to promote their digital assets, services, or profiles directly within the Archivehubs ecosystem.

Key Features:

1. Ad Objective Selection

Choose the goal of your campaign:

  • Promote a digital asset
  • Drive traffic to your profile or service
  • Increase visibility for a project or training
  • Recruit for a ateam or job

2. Ad Format Options

Select your preferred ad format:

  • Image ad (banner or square)
  • Video Ad (15 - 60 seconds)
  • Text Ad (headline & short description)
  • Carousel Ad (multiple digital assets or visuals)

3. Target Audience Customization

Set who sees your ad by defining:

  • Loctaion (e.g. city, region, country)
  • Industry/Interest categories
  • Profession level (e.g. feeelancer, researcher, student)
  • Language preference

4. Schedule & Duration

Choose:

  • Ad start & end dates
  • Daily or lifetime budget
  • Delivery speed (standard or accelerated)

5. Budget & Bidding

  • Set your daily or campaign budget
  • Choose bidding type:
    • Cost-per-click (CPC)
    • Cost-per-impression (CPM)

6. Performnce Insights

Track real-time metrics:

  • Views
  • Clicks
  • Engagement rate
  • Conversion actions (downloads, sales, profile visits)

7. Boost Option

  • Instantly boost your most viewed or recently uploaded asset
  • One-click promotion with default settings

How to Create your Ad:

navigation Go to:

1. Monetization Hub > Ads > Create Ad

2. Select Objective, Adience & Format

3. Upload your content and set your budget

4. Review, preview, then click Launch Ad

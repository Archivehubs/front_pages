1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your devices when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, improve performance, and personalize your experience.

    These are required for the operation of the website and enable core functions such as account login, secure browsing, and file access.

  • Functional Cookie

    Used to remember your preferences, such as language settings, dark mode, or saved filters.

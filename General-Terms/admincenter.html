Admin Center

Control. Customize. Coordinate

The Admin Center is the central control ub for managing organizational accounts, team activities, permissions, analytics, and integrationss on Archivehubs.com It empowers administrators to streamline digital operations, ensure security, and maximize productivity across teams or enterprise users.

Key Features of the Admin Center

User & Team Management

  • Add or remove team members
  • Assign roles (Admin, Editor, Viewer)
  • Set access level for digital assets and projects

Content & Asset Oversight

  • Monitor uploaded assets, folders, and categories
  • Approve or restrict shared content
  • Tag and archive files across departments

Analytics & Activity Reports

  • View usage statistics and engagement trends
  • Track asset downloads, sales, and views
  • Generate performance reports by team or individual

Workflow Automation

  • Set rules for asset approval and tagging
  • Automate folder organization and user notifications
  • Schedule regular backups or content reviews

Integration & API Controls

  • Manage third-party tool integrations (e.g, Google Drive, payment gateways)
  • Access API keys and permissions
  • Set webhooks for real-time updates

Notification & Security Settings

  • Configure account alerts and admin-only notifications
  • Enable 2FA, IP restrictions, and data encryption
  • Audit activity logs and detect unusual behavior

Billing & Subscription Management

  • Manage payment methods and invoices
  • Upgrade plans or add seats
  • Access historical billing records

Designed for:

  • Enterprise Teams
  • Educational Institutions
  • Content Agencies
  • Remote Collaborators
  • Marketplace Managers
  • Organizations

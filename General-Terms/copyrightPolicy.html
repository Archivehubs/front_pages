If you believe that your copyrighted work has been used on Archivehubs.com without authorization, please submit a written notice with the following information:

  • Your name and contact details
  • A description f the copyrighted work being infringed.
  • A direct URL or location of the allegedly infringing content.
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is unauthorized.
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the information is accurate and you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.
  • Your electronic or physical signature.

If you believe your content was wrongly removed due to a copyright complaint, you may file a counter-notice that includes:

  • Your contact information.
  • Identification of the removed content and its original URL.
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you believe the content was removed in error.
  • Your consent to jurisdiction in your country or region and willingness to accept service of process.

Send counter-notices to:

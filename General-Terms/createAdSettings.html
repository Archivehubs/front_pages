Create Ad Settings

These settings help advertisers tailor, optimize, and manage their ads to suit different goals, budgets, and audiences.

1. Campaign Objective

Choose your campaign goal:

  • Promote Digital Assets
  • Drive Profile or Page Traffic
  • Advertise a Service or Event
  • Recruit or Collaborate
  • Increase Marketplace Visibility

2. Target Audience

Define who sees your ad:

  • Location: Country, State, City
  • Age Range: Custom or default brackets
  • Interest Categories: (e.g. Education, Design, Research)
  • Profession Level: Student, Freelacer, Professional
  • Language Preferences: Primary ad langugae

3. Ad Format

Select how your ad appears:

  • Image Ad (Static banners)
  • Video Ad (Short-form clips)
  • Text-Only Ad (Headlines nd Call-to-Action)
  • Carousel Ad (Multi-asset showcase)

4. Ad Duration & Schedule

Set when and how long your ad will run:

  • Start & End Dates
  • Time of Day Display (e.g. business hours only)
  • Recurring Schedule Option (weekly/monthly)

5. Budget & Bidding

Customize your ad spend:

  • Daily budget or lifetime budget
  • Bidding strategy:
    • CPC (Cost per click)
    • CPM (Cost per 100 impressions)
    • Manual vs. automated bidding

6. Placement Options

Choose where your ad is shown on Archivehubs:

  • Homepage Feeds
  • Search Results
  • Asset Pages
  • Talent or Job boards
  • Inside Team or Project Hubs

7. Performnance Tracking

Enable tracking for:

  • Impressions, clicks, and CTR
  • Downloads or Conversions
  • Engagement Time
  • ROI & Spending breakdown

8. Auto Optimization (Optional)

  • Turn on to let the platform adjust ad placements and targeting for better performance
  • Set max bid increase/decrease percentages

9. Ad Notifications

Choose how to be notified:

  • Campaign status updates (Live, Rejected, Completed)
  • Performance summaries (Daily/Weekly Reports)
  • Budget limit alerts

10. Ad Privacy & Visibility

Control who cn see your ad:

  • Public (Default)
  • Professionals Only
  • Niche Networks (e.g. Design Community, Researchers)
  • Custom Groups

Access Path:

navigation Navigate to:

Monetization Hub > Ads > Create Ad > Settings

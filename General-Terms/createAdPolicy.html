Create Ad Policy

1. Eligibility

To create ads on Archivehubs.com users must:

  • Have a verified account
  • Have a Corporate Page
  • Comply with our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines

2. Permitted Ad Content

You may promote:

  • Digital assets (e.g courses, certificates, PRDs, research)
  • Services (e.g design, photography, financial consultation)
  • Job or talent opportunities
  • Tutorials, webinars,or training materials
  • Portfolio or user profile pages
  • Marketplace activities and products

3. Prohibited Ad Content

The following are not allowed in any ads:

  • False, misleading, or deceptive claims
  • Adult content or nudity
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Weapons, violence, or illegal activities
  • Unlicensed or copyrighted materials
  • Get-rich-quick schemes or MLMs

4. Ad Format Guidelines

  • Images must be clear, relevant, and properly sized (no distorted or low-resolution graphics)
  • Videos Must not autoplay with sund or contain flashing content
  • Text must be gramatically correct and not include excessive punctuation or ALL CAPS
  • Landing pages must match the ad content

5. Targeting Standards

Advertisers must:

  • Use targeting tools ethically
  • Avoid targeting minors or vulnerable groups unless appropriate
  • Not use sensitive personal information for targeting (e.g. health or political data)

6. Review Process

  • All ads are subject to automated and manual review before publication
  • Review may take up to 24 hours
  • Ads violating policies may be rejected or removed with or without notice

7. Transparency Requirements

Ads must:

  • Clearly identify the sponsor or business behind the promotion
  • Include any necessary disclaimers or complaince info (especially for financial, medical, or educational services)

8. Consequences of Violation

Violations may result in:

  • Rejection or removal of the ad
  • Temporary or permanent suspension of ad privileges
  • Account review or termination in severe cases

9. Appeals & Feedback

  • You can appeal a rejected ad via the Ad Manager > Disputes tab
  • Feedback will be provided within 48 hours

10. Policy Updates

Archivehubs.com may update this policy periodically. Users will be notified of material changes, and continued use implies agreement to the new terms.

For questions or further clarification, contact:

adsupport@archivehubs.com
